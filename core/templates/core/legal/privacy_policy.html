Privacy Policy (Draft)

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Elevo collects only the data needed to deliver learning, assessment, and interview preparation services for students and partner organizations.

We process account data, course/interview activity, submissions, and optional uploaded files (for example resumes) to provide product functionality and analytics.

Organization administrators may access learner progress and usage metrics for users associated with their organization.

Elevo does not sell personal data. Data is shared only with infrastructure and service providers required to operate the platform.

Users and organizations can request data export and deletion in line with applicable contract terms and legal obligations.

This document is a draft and should be reviewed by legal counsel before commercial rollout.
