# Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: March 3, 2026

## Scope
This draft applies to Elevo services used by individuals and subscribing educational organizations.

## Data Collected
- Account identity and authentication data
- Learning activity (coding submissions, aptitude attempts, interview sessions)
- Optional files uploaded by users (for example resumes)
- Organization membership and plan metadata

## Purposes
- Service delivery and user authentication
- Learning analytics and progress reporting
- Product reliability, abuse prevention, and support

## Data Sharing
Elevo shares data only with subprocessors necessary to host and operate the platform.

## Retention and Deletion
Data retention periods are based on product use, contractual obligations, and legal requirements.  
Organizations may request export and deletion workflows under contract.

## Draft Status
This is a draft and requires legal review before production commercial use.
