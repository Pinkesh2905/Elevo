# Data Processing Addendum (DPA) Draft

Effective date: March 3, 2026

## Roles
- Customer organization: Data Controller
- Elevo: Data Processor

## Processing Scope
Elevo processes student and organization data needed to deliver platform functions, analytics, and support.

## Security Controls
Elevo applies technical and organizational controls appropriate to service risk, including encrypted transport and access controls.

## Subprocessors
Elevo uses subprocessors for hosting, email, and AI infrastructure where required.

## Incident Handling
Elevo will notify customer organizations of confirmed security incidents in accordance with contract and law.

## Data Subject Rights
Elevo supports controller requests for access, deletion, and export where feasible and legally permitted.

## Draft Status
This is a draft and requires legal review before production commercial use.
