# Elevo Week 1 Pricing Draft (Institution-Focused)

## Institutional Plans

### Starter
- Price: INR 14,999 / month
- Seats: up to 120 students
- Coding problems: up to 250
- Aptitude quizzes: 5/day/student
- Mock interviews: 3/month/student
- Features:
  - editorials enabled
  - detailed analytics disabled
  - custom branding disabled
  - priority support disabled

### Growth
- Price: INR 39,999 / month
- Seats: up to 500 students
- Coding problems: unlimited
- Aptitude quizzes: unlimited
- Mock interviews: 10/month/student
- Features:
  - editorials enabled
  - detailed analytics enabled
  - custom branding enabled
  - priority support disabled

### Enterprise
- Price: INR 99,999 / month
- Seats: unlimited
- Coding problems: unlimited
- Aptitude quizzes: unlimited
- Mock interviews: unlimited
- Features:
  - editorials enabled
  - detailed analytics enabled
  - custom branding enabled
  - priority support enabled

## Additional Plans

### Free Trial (Organization)
- Price: INR 0
- Seats: 25
- Intended only for evaluation onboarding

### Personal Pro (Individual)
- Price: INR 499 / month
- Unlimited coding/aptitude + 10 interviews/month

## Commercial Notes
- Final pricing should be validated by pilot outcomes and support load.
- Enterprise contracts can include annual billing and onboarding services.
